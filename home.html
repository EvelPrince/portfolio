About My Art

My art is greatly inspired by the media I have consumed growing up.
I have always loved film, especially content from the 80's era.
I am also greatly attracted to jewel tones and strong contrasting color palettes.
Music plays a big theme in the emotions behind the pieces I create as well;
certain soundtracks help create the tone of different pieces.
I'm sure as I continue to learn and grow, my tastes and influences will change as well.
