Digital Illustrations; 2022

"Bodily Autonomy" and "Hollow Self" are both illustrations that are types of self portraits for me. As a person with disabilities, a sense of self is hard to articulate, so I try and achieve it through my art.

"Age" and "Oni" are both character designs I worked on with the focus being the colors and lighting. The "Age" piece is from earlier in the year and "Oni" is more recent. You can see the improvement in the line-work and composition.
